Enterprise Use Cases

Security & Data Protection

Physically connected but logically isolated. Keep sensitive backup drives offline to protect against ransomware and unauthorized access while maintaining rapid restoration capability.

System Maintenance

Safely prepare disks for maintenance or replacement. Ensure files are flushed and IO is ceased through the application's built-in validation before taking storage offline.

Testing & Development

Simulate disk failures or environment changes by toggling drive availability. Ideal for storage application developers and QA engineers testing failure recovery.

Power User Efficiency

Avoid digging through Disk Management (Windows) or complex `mount` commands (Linux). A unified, high-performance interface for all disk operations.
